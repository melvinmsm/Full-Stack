Indori Poha

Poha is an easy, delicious and healthy breakfast recipe, popular in Indore. Made with onions, potatoes and seasoning like chillies, lemon and curry leaves make up a tasty and easy meal of Poha, especially when you don't feel like cooking.

Total time:- 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Poha
  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • 1/2 cup Onions, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp Lemon juice

Preparation

  1. Put poha in a colander and wash, not letting it soak too long in the water. Leave in the colander to drain out completely.

    15 minutes

  2. Heat the oil and add the asafoetida, mustard seeds, curry leaves and onions along with whole red chillies.

    10 minutes

  3. When onions are light brown, add potatoes, and turn around till they look a bit glossy.

    5 minutes
